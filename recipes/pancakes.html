Grain-Free Chocolate Chip Pecan Pancakes

My recipe is fluffier than whatever these are. Just sayin'.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup buckwheat flour
  • 1/2 cup cassava flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 4 tbsp unsalted butter or ghee
  • 1 1/2 cup unsweetened vanilla coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 1/2-2 tbsp coconut sugar
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2-1 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup pecans, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Combine the milk and apple cider vinegar and whisk vigorously. Set aside for at least 5 mins
  2. Combine the flours, sugar, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Beat eggs into the milk mixture. Add vanilla extract.
  4. Melt butter or ghee until liquid.
  5. Combine wet ingredients with dry ingredients, then add pecans and chocolate chips. Whisk together just until ingredients are blended. Do not overmix.
  6. Heat coconut oil on a pan and fry pancakes at desired size.
