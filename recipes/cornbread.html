Gluten-Free Cornbread

nope, it doesn't crumble

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups yellow cornmeal
  • 1 cup Bob's Red Mill 1 to 1 baking flour
  • 2 cups Oatly Full Fat oat milk
  • 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tsp sea salt
  • 3 tbsp coconut sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/3 cup ghee & olive oil blend
  • optional: 1 jalapeno

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. If using jalapeno, dice as small as possible. Do not toss the seeds.
  3. Combine the milk and apple cider vinegar, whisk vigorously. Add jalapeno if using and stir. Set aside for at least 5 minutes.
  4. Combine the flours, sugar, salt, baking powder, and baking soda.
  5. Melt the ghee and olive oil mixture in the microwave until liquid (10-15 seconds).
  6. Add oil to the dry ingredients, then add the milk mixture. Whisk together until thoroughly blended. Pour into an 8x8 baking dish.
  7. Bake for 35 minutes or until a knife comes out clean.
