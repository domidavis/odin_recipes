Grain-Free All-Butter Pie Crust

This assignment is making me hungry. :(

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cassava flour
  • 1 cup buckwheat flour
  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 2 tsp coconut sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 16 tbsp unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup ice water

Steps

  1. Mix flours, sugar, and salt together in a food processor.
  2. Add cubed butter and process.
  3. Gradually add water 2 tbsps at a time. Process after each addition.
  4. Press dough into a ball, cut in half.
  5. Mold each half into flat-ish discs. Don't let the butter start melting! Wrap each disc in plastic and chill in fridge for at least 2 hrs.
  6. Use for any pie or quiche recipe you choose!
